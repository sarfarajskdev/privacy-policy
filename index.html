Privacy Policy

1. Where This Policy Applies

This Privacy Policy applies to the UnitConvertor mobile application, available on the Apple App Store. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

UnitConvertor is a simple offline utility app. It does not require you to create an account, and it does not collect any personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Your conversion history or usage data
  • Any financial or payment information

All unit conversions are performed entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server or third party.

3. How the App Works

The App functions completely offline. You do not need an internet connection to use UnitConvertor. No data leaves your device during normal use of the App.

Any preferences saved by the App, such as your last used unit settings, are stored locally on your device only and are not accessible to us.

4. Apple App Store and Analytics

When you download the App through the Apple App Store, Apple may collect certain information as part of their standard platform operations. This data collection is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy, not ours.

If you have opted into sharing App analytics with developers through your device settings, Apple may share aggregated and anonymized crash data or usage statistics with us. This data does not personally identify you in any way.

5. Third-Party Services

UnitConvertor does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. There are no advertisements in the App, and no third party receives any data from your use of the App.

6. Children's Privacy

UnitConvertor is a safe, educational utility app suitable for all ages, including young children. The App contains no inappropriate content, no advertisements, and collects no personal data from anyone. It is designed to be a helpful learning tool for students and users of all ages.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal information at risk on our end. The App operates locally on your device, which is protected by your device's built-in security features such as your passcode and Face ID.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide access to, correct, or delete. If you have any privacy concerns, you are welcome to contact us at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
